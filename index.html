Annanya Gera Portfolios

“Do I contradict myself? Very well then I contradict myself, (I am large, I contain multitudes)”

Hello! I'm Annanya Gera, and I’m passionate about expressing myself through creativity. Music and poetry have always been two important outlets for me, offering ways to explore emotions and thoughts in unique ways. I enjoy composing melodies and writing verses that reflect my inner world. Alongside my love for the arts, I’ve recently delved into the world of web development. Learning how to design and code websites has opened up a new form of creativity, and I’m excited to keep growing my skills in this area. My goal is to merge my artistic interests with tech, creating meaningful and beautiful digital experiences.
